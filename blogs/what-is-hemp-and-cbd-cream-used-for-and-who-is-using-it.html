What is Hemp and CBD cream used for and who is using it?

Author: D. Astor

Published: March 23, 2020

There is a lot of talk about Hemp and CBD products and the benefits these days. Well-known professional athletes are reporting using CBD products for the natural benefits provided from the ingredient.

One repeated comment has been the interest in using the product for its relief for sore and injured muscles. Amongst the most popular are the topical relief rubs, roll-ons, and topical sprays.

Another strong consumer group has been seniors, as people grow older they begin to feel the stress on joints, muscles, neck, and backaches. Many older consumers swear by CBD and say it has improved their life. As we researched what others were saying we also came upon an interesting consumer group which was a close second with the older community, moms. Mothers with newborns and school-aged children seem to have a lot of body stresses, not that we didn't realize the tasks are daunting but we had no idea how big of a focus was on this category of shopper who is in search of relief from life's everyday aches and pains.

Moms seem to be very interested in natural products and certainly are hyperfocused on maintaining a healthy lifestyle, so CBD topicals have become their go-to for a daily solution.

Overall, what we have found is that CBD is quickly becoming a common product you might find in any household.
